Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 3, 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you create an account, we collect your name and email address. As you use Spendly, we store the expense records you create — including amounts, categories, dates, and any descriptions you provide. We do not collect payment information, location data, or any data beyond what you explicitly enter.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide the Spendly service — displaying your expense history, generating summaries, and enabling you to manage your records. We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond operating the app. We will never sell your personal information to third parties.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored on secure servers. We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect it against unauthorized access, loss, or disclosure. Your account is protected by the password you set; please use a strong, unique password and keep it confidential. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly does not currently share your data with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. If this changes in the future, we will update this policy and notify you in advance. The app may use Google Fonts to load typefaces; this request goes to Google's servers but does not include any of your personal or expense data.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out to us at privacy@spendly.app. We will respond within a reasonable timeframe.
